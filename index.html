cv 106 KB artist statement Carlos A. Correia’s solo practice explores the private space by investigating individual identity in relation to collective identity within political and social contexts, both local and global. Using repetition as a core technique, his work delves deeply into themes of labor, hierarchy, and social norms. Often functioning as a critical commentary on contemporary societal structures, Carlos’ art invites reflection on the complexities of human experience. His work has been exhibited internationally, with a focus on Norway and Portugal, and is included in several collections.
